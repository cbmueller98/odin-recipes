Penne and Beef

Description

Beef penne is an easy and delicious weeknight dinner. Penne noodles with ground beef and your favorite pasta sauce.

Ingredients

  • Whole grain penne pasta
  • Ground chuck
  • Pasta sauce
  • Red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Boil pasta for 10 minutes in salted water. Stir occasionally and drain thoroughly when done.
  2. Brown beef on medium high in a non-stick pan. Drain thoroughly and season with salt, pepper, garlic and onion.
  3. Pour your pasta sauce into the pan and mix in with beef over medium heat until sauce is warm.
  4. Serve meat sauce over noodles with plenty of red pepper flakes.
